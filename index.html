 About the Game

Where Magic Meets Learning

 Unicorns: Puzzle Game for Kids is a vibrant puzzle app designed to promote developmental growth through visual, interactive fun. Children match and place puzzle pieces to reveal charming unicorn scenes while strengthening cognitive abilities.

  • Designed for ages 2-7
  • Adorable hand-drawn unicorns
  • Teaches problem-solving and hand-eye coordination

How It Works

Just Tap, Drag, and Place!

  • Kids choose a puzzle image
  • Drag the correct pieces into place
  • A completed unicorn scene triggers a fun animation and applause
  • Advance to more pieces or a new image

The game adjusts in complexity so your child grows confidently with every completed puzzle!
